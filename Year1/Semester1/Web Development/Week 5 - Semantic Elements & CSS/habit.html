Gentle temperament: Alaskan malamute dogs are “gentle giants” and they are friendly to everyone, but especially towards the kids. They have a big heart and enough love to go around. One of the personality traits of Alaskan malamute dogs is patience. They allow kids to jump on them and to play with them a lot.
