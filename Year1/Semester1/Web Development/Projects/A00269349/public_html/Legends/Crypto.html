Crypto

Crypto

Details

Real Name: Tae Joon Park
Gender: Male
Age: Unknown

Background

Crypto specializes in secrets; he knows how to uncover them, and how to keep them. A brilliant hacker and encryption expert, he uses aerial drones to spy on his opponents in the Apex Arena without being seen. Itâ€™s been noted that his drones have a similar design to those created by wanted murderer Tae Joon Park.

Orphans raised on the streets of Suotamo, Tae Joon and his foster sister Mila Alexander escaped a life of squalor by becoming computer engineers, designing drones that transmit the Apex Games throughout the Outlands. But one day, Tae Joon disappeared with an important company secret, and his sister vanished under mysterious and violent circumstances.

The truth however is that a program they accidentally discovered, a prediction algorithm for the Apex Games that can be used to game the betting systems and gain them a ton of money, was what led to the tragic events surrounding Mila's disappearance and Tae Joon's framing for murder. On the run from the Syndicate, he gave up his identity, his face and his family in order to facilitate his redemption. He is now Crypto and in the games, he now seeks justice

His first move in his redemption plan was blowing up the Repulsor Tower using an EMP blast, allowing the entry of hostile wildlife on Kings Canyon and disrupting the games.

Gameplay

A Recon Legend, Crypto takes to the skies with his Surveillance Drone, revealing enemies for both Crypto and his teammates, thanks to Neural Link. And if he finds that the enemies have already hunkered down, he can activate his Drone EMP to break their defenses and slow them, before he comes out of the shadows and makes his move with his squad.

Tactical ABILITY

Surveillance Drone

Deploy an aerial drone that allows you to view the surrounding area from above. If the drone is destroyed, there is a forty-second cooldown before you can deploy another.

Passive ABILITY

Neurolink

Enemies detected by the Surveillance Drone within 30 meters of your position are marked for you and your teammates to see


Ultimate ABILITY

Drone Emp

Your Surveillance Drone sets off an EMP blast that deals shield damage, slows enemies, and disables traps.
