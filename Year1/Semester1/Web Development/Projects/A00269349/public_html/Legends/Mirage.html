Mirage

Mirage

Details

Real Name: Elliott Witt
Gender: Male
Age: 30

Background

Mirage is the kind of guy who likes to stand out. The youngest of four brothers, he perfected the art of fooling around to get attention. The one thing he took seriously was Holo-Pilot technology: introduced to the illusion-creating tech by his engineer mother, he pored over the mechanisms and learned all he could about them. Even when his brothers went missing during the Frontier War, Mirage and his mother continued to develop holo devices, and the work brought them closer.

While working as a bartender on the Paradise Lounge in Solace City[1] to make some extra money, Mirage heard amazing stories from his patrons about the Apex Games and the wealth and glory that came with victory. As good as both of those sounded, he knew he couldn't risk leaving his mother childless â€“ until she gave him a set of customized holo devices and told him to follow his dream. Mirage is now the life of the Apex Games, outwitting opponents and charming audiences across the Outlands.

Gameplay

Mirage is a more difficult Offensive Legend to play, but is useful for distraction and revealing. His Decoys can confuse enemies, and, when they shoot them, reveals to Mirage the exact position of the enemy. His Ultimate drops a lot of decoys and makes him invisible, letting him confuse enemies, ping them for his team, and then outflank and outlive the enemy. If Mirage gets in over his head and gets knocked down,he just turns invisible to ping the enemy and wait for his team to revive to continue his job.

Tactical ABILITY

Psyche Out

Send out a holographic decoy to confuse the enemy.


Passive ABILITY

Encore!

Automatically drop a decoy and cloak for five seconds when knocked down.

Ultimate ABILITY

Vanishing Act

Deploy a team of Decoys to distract enemies while you cloak.
